How to install this as an app

1. Open this file in Safari
Must be Safari, not Chrome or Firefox
2. Tap the Share button
The box with an arrow pointing up, bottom center
3. Tap "Add to Home Screen"
Scroll down in the share sheet if needed
4. Tap "Add"
It will appear on your home screen like any app
Important: always export before clearing Safari data or switching phones. Your records live only on this device.
